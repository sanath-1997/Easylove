# **App Name**: Marry Her: 10 Steps Ebook Landing

## Core Features:

- Ebook Content Display: Showcase the ebook description, benefits, and detailed step outlines, ensuring clear communication of its value.
- Visual Appeal Enhancement: Utilize a captivating landing page design to maintain user attention and improve the likelihood of ebook purchases.
- Dynamic Offer Countdown: Implement a countdown timer to highlight the time-sensitive nature of the ebook offer, thus encouraging immediate purchases.
- Purchase Interface: Provide a straightforward payment process, encouraging worry-free access and facilitating quick purchase decisions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Passionate crimson (#DC143C), drawing on the concepts of love, romance, and intensity from the prompt.
- Background color: Light pink (#F9E4E8), a gentle, desaturated tone providing a soft backdrop that harmonizes with the primary color.
- Accent color: A vivid magenta (#FF00FF) is selected for high contrast and to highlight key engagement points, evoking energy.
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a humanist sans-serif aligning with the design and the content itself.
- Body font: 'Alegreya', a serif font with an elegant, intellectual, contemporary feel to provide easily legible blocks of text.
- Incorporate heart and love-themed icons to visually reinforce the book's message.
- Structure content with clear sections for easy navigation.
- Incorporate subtle animations, particularly on the price drop and countdown timer, to capture user attention.
